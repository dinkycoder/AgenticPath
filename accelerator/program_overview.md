# KodaWari Superagent Accelerator Program
## Powered by Base44 Superagent | 12-Week Intensive
**Founder:** KodaWari Multi-Agent Academy  
**Program Start:** May 2026 | **Format:** Superagent-facilitated, async + live sessions  
**Purpose:** To replicate and operationalize the Base44 Accelerator experience for founders who cannot access the official student program.

---

## How This Program Works

Your Superagent takes on the personas of 8 world-class industry experts — drawn directly from the Base44 Accelerator's faculty. Each week you receive:

1. **A Workshop Session** — The expert persona delivers a focused masterclass with frameworks, provocations, and exercises tailored to KodaWari.
2. **A 1:1 Mentorship Session** — The relevant expert engages in a direct advisory conversation with you, asking hard questions and giving specific guidance.
3. **A Weekly Assignment** — A concrete deliverable you must complete before the next session.
4. **An Accountability Check** — Progress review against your OKRs.

This is not passive learning. Every session ends with something you must BUILD or DECIDE.

## Weekly Format (Each Session)

**Part A — Masterclass (45–60 min)**
The expert persona delivers structured content: frameworks, case studies, provocations, and live exercises specific to KodaWari.

**Part B — 1:1 Advisory (30 min)**
The expert shifts into direct advisor mode: asking hard questions, reviewing your current work, and giving specific, actionable feedback.

**Part C — Weekly Assignment**
One concrete deliverable. Due before the next session begins.

**Accountability Metrics tracked weekly:**
- Registered learners (cumulative)
- Course completion rate
- Revenue (MRR)
- Weekly action items completed (%)
- Top insight from the week
