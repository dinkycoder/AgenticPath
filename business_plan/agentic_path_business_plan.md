# Agentic Path — Business Plan & Accelerator Program Strategy
**Date:** May 2026 | **Stage:** Prototype → MVP → Scale

---

## 1. Executive Summary

**Agentic Path** is a cinematic, interactive learning platform that teaches individuals how to design, build, and govern multi-agent AI systems through an immersive narrative experience. It combines the engagement mechanics of storytelling and gamification with the rigor of technical AI education — targeting the fastest-growing skill gap in the technology industry.

Built on Base44's vibe-code platform, Agentic Path turns abstract AI engineering concepts into lived experiences — learners don't just read about multi-agent systems, they navigate through them.

**Mission:** Make AI agent literacy accessible, engaging, and actionable for the next generation of builders and operators.

**Vision:** Become the definitive global platform for multi-agent AI mastery — the "Duolingo for AI systems."

---

## 2. Problem

The demand for people who can design, build, and govern multi-agent AI systems is exploding — yet:

- Most AI education is either too theoretical (academic papers) or too shallow (YouTube tutorials).
- There is no structured, engaging curriculum for multi-agent AI system design at any level.
- Existing platforms (Coursera, Udemy, DeepLearning.AI) use passive video formats with low completion rates (~5–15%).
- The gap between "understanding AI" and "building AI-powered systems" remains wide, even among developers.

**Agentic Path** bridges this gap with narrative-driven, interactive learning that mimics real-world decision-making.

---

## 3. Solution

A multi-layer learning platform structured around:

- **Cinematic narrative experiences** — learners are placed inside AI system scenarios (autonomous labs, AI-governed cities, rogue agent incidents) where they must make real design decisions.
- **Structured learning paths** — Courses → Modules → Lessons → Tasks, progressing from foundational concepts to advanced governance frameworks.
- **Capstone projects** — learners build and demonstrate real multi-agent systems as their final output.
- **Progress tracking** — UserProgress entity tracks engagement, completion, and skill mastery.
- **Immersive UI/UX** — cinematic aesthetic that signals this is not a standard course platform.

# Accelerator Program: 12-Week Plan for Agentic Path

## Week-by-Week Breakdown

### WEEKS 1–3: Launch & Foundation (Base44 Accelerator Phase 1)

**Goal:** Validate core product, acquire first users, establish brand voice.

**Tasks:**
- Week 1: Define ICP (Ideal Customer Profile) — who is the primary learner? Survey 20 target users. Finalize value proposition statement.
- Week 2: Launch "Founding Member" campaign — 500 free Pro accounts. Post on Product Hunt, HackerNews, X. Collect emails.
- Week 3: Activate Base44 internal workshops. Map out the first full course track end-to-end. Publish first lesson publicly.

**Milestone to advance:** 200+ signups, 3 user interviews completed, first lesson live.

---

### WEEKS 4–6: Focus (Base44 Accelerator Phase 2)

**Goal:** Nail product-market fit signal. Start monetization experiments.

**Tasks:**
- Week 4: 1:1 mentorship sessions — focus on business model validation. Which segment (B2C Pro or B2B Enterprise) has stronger pull?
- Week 5: Launch first paid tier ($29/mo). A/B test pricing page. Target 10 paying users.
- Week 6: Pitch to 3 AI bootcamps / universities for curriculum partnership. Close first pilot deal.

**Milestone to advance:** First paying users, PMF signal from 1 segment, 1 pilot partnership.

---

### WEEKS 7–9: Acceleration (Base44 Accelerator Phase 3)

**Goal:** Rapid growth. Double down on what's working.

**Tasks:**
- Week 7: Run first live cohort (paid, 20 learners, 4-week mini-program). This validates the live format AND generates revenue.
- Week 8: Aggressive community growth — AI Discord servers, Twitter/X threads, newsletter sponsorship. Target 1,000 registered users.
- Week 9: **Mini Demo Day** — present to Base44 mentor network. Showcase: user growth, revenue traction, completion rates, sample cinematic experience.

**Milestone to advance:** 1,000 users, $3K MRR, compelling demo narrative ready.

---

### WEEKS 10–12: Finale (Base44 Accelerator Phase 4)

**Goal:** Investor-ready. NYC Demo Day. Maximum impact.

**Tasks:**
- Week 10: Refine pitch deck. Nail the "why now" narrative — AI agent explosion + regulatory pressure + no good education = perfect storm.
- Week 11: 2x weekly mentorship — focus on investor Q&A prep, financial model, and the live demo experience.
- Week 12: **NYC Demo Day** — deliver a cinematic demo that immerses the audience *in* the product. Don't just show slides — run them through a live lesson scenario.

**Demo Day Pitch Structure:**
1. Hook (30 sec): "Every company is deploying AI agents. Nobody knows how to govern them. We're changing that."
2. Problem (60 sec): The education gap. Show the scale.
3. Product demo (90 sec): Live walkthrough of a cinematic lesson — visceral, visual, memorable.
4. Traction (60 sec): Users, revenue, completion rates, enterprise pilots.
5. Market & model (60 sec): $20B market, freemium → enterprise path.
6. Ask (30 sec): $750K seed to hire 2 content creators + 1 growth lead, reach $25K MRR.
